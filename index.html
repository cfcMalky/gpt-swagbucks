FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Is Swagbucks actually legit?

Yes — Swagbucks has been operating since 2008 and paid over $950 million to 20M+ users globally. It's owned by Prodege, LLC (which also runs InboxDollars and MyPoints), employs around 250 people, and holds a B rating with the Better Business Bureau. With 42,000+ Trustpilot reviews at 4.2★, it's the most-reviewed rewards platform in the world.

How does the $10 sign-up bonus work?

After registering, go to the Swag Ups section and activate the $10 bonus offer. To claim it, make a qualifying $25 purchase through the Swagbucks shopping portal within 30 days of signing up. If you'd rather not spend money upfront, you can skip the activation and earn through surveys, offers, and games instead — the bonus is optional and never required.

What's the minimum payout?

For gift cards, the minimum is just $3 (300 SB) for options like Amazon — making it easy to get your first reward quickly. For PayPal cash, the minimum is $25 (2,500 SB). Gift cards are therefore a much faster path to your first redemption, especially when you're building up your initial balance.

How long does a payout take?

Standard payouts — gift cards and PayPal — take 2–5 business days. Reach Topaz status (by accumulating enough lifetime SB points) and you unlock real-time instant gift card delivery. PayPal cash always processes within 2–5 days regardless of your membership level.

Is Swagbucks available outside the US?

Yes — Swagbucks is available globally. However, earning potential is highest in the US. International members (including UK) access surveys, some cashback shopping via local partner stores, and game offers. Not all Discover offers or Swago bonuses may be available in every country. Check your region's available offers after signing up to gauge what's accessible to you.

Why do I keep getting screened out of surveys?

Survey disqualifications are Swagbucks' most common complaint — and industry-wide, not just here. Market research companies target specific demographics, so if your profile doesn't match a survey's required audience, you get screened out. Tip: complete your profile fully, and treat surveys as a secondary earner rather than your main income method. Discover offers and cashback shopping are more reliable.

Is there a Swagbucks mobile app?

Yes — Swagbucks has both an iOS app (Swagbucks: Surveys For Money) and an Android app (Swagbucks Play Games + Surveys). The app includes mobile-exclusive offers like SB LIVE trivia and mobile game offers not available on desktop. Downloading the app meaningfully expands your earning options, particularly for game milestone offers.

How does the referral program work?

Share your referral link. When a friend earns 300 SB in their first month, you both get a bonus. More valuably: you earn 10% of everything they earn — forever. There's no time limit and no cap on referrals. Refer someone who stays active for years and that 10% passive commission compounds significantly over time.
